Privacy Policy

At Skyvora LLP, we highly value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We understand that trust is the foundation of every successful journey, and we take every reasonable measure to ensure that your information remains safe, secure, and confidential.

Information We Collect

To provide our travel services efficiently, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • WhatsApp Number
  • Email Address
  • Residential Address (if required)
  • Travel Destination
  • Travel Dates
  • Number of Travelers
  • Co-Traveler Details
  • Emergency Contact Information (if required)
  • Identity Documents (where applicable)
  • Payment-related Information

How We Use Your Information

  • Processing and confirming travel bookings.
  • Coordinating hotel, flight, transport, and other reservations.
  • Sending booking confirmations, itineraries, invoices, and updates.
  • Providing travel assistance and customer support.
  • Meeting legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Improving our travel services and customer experience.

Confidentiality of Customer Information

All customer information is treated as strictly confidential.

Skyvora LLP does not sell, rent, trade, or disclose your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Your information is shared only with trusted travel partners such as airlines, hotels, transport providers, visa agencies, and payment partners solely for completing your travel arrangements.

Only authorized Skyvora employees who require access to your information for official work are permitted to handle your data.

Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or loss.

  • Secure storage of customer information.
  • Restricted access to authorized staff only.
  • Industry-standard security practices.
  • Continuous monitoring to improve data protection.

Although no digital system is 100% secure, we continuously strive to provide the highest level of protection for your personal information.

Communication

By providing your contact information, you authorize Skyvora to communicate with you through:

  • Phone Calls
  • WhatsApp
  • SMS
  • Email

Communication may include booking confirmations, travel reminders, payment updates, emergency notifications, and customer support.

Third-Party Services

Certain travel services are provided by independent third-party partners such as airlines, hotels, transportation providers, visa agencies, insurance companies, and payment gateways.

Each partner follows its own privacy policy. Skyvora LLP is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party providers.

Customer Rights

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Update your contact details.
  • Understand how your data is used.
  • Request deletion of personal information where legally permitted.

Policy Updates

Skyvora LLP reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy whenever required to comply with legal obligations or improve our services.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact our Customer Support Team or your assigned Sales Representative.

At Skyvora LLP, protecting your personal information is one of our highest priorities. We are committed to handling your data responsibly, securely, and with complete transparency throughout your travel experience.
